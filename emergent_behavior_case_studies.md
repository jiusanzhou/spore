# Case Studies and Examples of Emergent Behavior in Swarm Robotics and AI Systems

## Executive Summary
This document compiles research on emergent behavior in swarm robotics and AI systems, covering classic case studies, recent advances, and practical applications.

## 1. Classic Swarm Robotics Case Studies

### Harvard Kilobot Swarm (2014)
- System: 1024 coin-sized robots with infrared communication
- Emergent Behavior: Self-assembly into complex 2D shapes
- Key Finding: Simple local rules create complex global patterns
- Publication: Science 345.6198 (2014): 795-799

### E-puck Robot Swarms
- Applications: Flocking, collective transport, formation control
- Emergent Behaviors: Coordinated motion without central control
- Research: Multiple European research institutions
